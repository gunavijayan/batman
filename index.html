Who is Batman?

Batman's secret indentity is Bruce Wayne, a wealthy American playboy, philanthropist, and owner of Wayne Enterprises. Batman originated from an incident in Bruce's childhood after witnessing his parents murder Thomas Wayne and Martha Wayne. He swore an vengeance against criminals, and oath tempered by a sense of justice. Bruce trains himself physically and intellectually and crafts a bat-inspired persona to fight crime.
Batman operates in the Gotham City with assistance from various supporting characters, including his butler Alfred, police commissioner Jim Gordan, and vigilante allies such as Robin and Catwoman.
